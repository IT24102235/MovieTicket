About CineApp

We're transforming the movie-going experience with innovative technology and passion for cinema

Our Story

Founded in 2019, CineApp was born out of frustration with the traditional movie ticket booking process. Our founders, avid movie enthusiasts, believed there had to be a better way to connect audiences with the magic of cinema.

What started as a small startup with a handful of partner theaters has now grown into a nationwide platform serving millions of movie lovers. Our journey has been defined by innovation, perseverance, and an unwavering commitment to enhancing the cinema experience.

Today, CineApp is the preferred movie ticketing platform for over 500 theaters nationwide, offering not just tickets but a complete cinema experienceâ€”from trailers and reviews to food ordering and exclusive deals.
