Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Refund policies vary by theater. In general, you can request a refund up to 2 hours before the movie starts. To request a refund, log into your account, go to "My Tickets," select the ticket and click the "Request Refund" button. Alternatively, you can contact our customer support team.

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters related to same-day bookings, we recommend calling our customer support line directly for immediate assistance.

Theater owners can partner with us by filling out the contact form and selecting "Business Partnership" as the subject. Our business development team will reach out with more information about our partnership program, integration options, and revenue-sharing models.
