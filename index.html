Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

Booking tickets is simple! Search for your desired movie or browse the 'Now Showing' section, select your preferred theater, date, and showtime. Choose your seats, complete the payment process, and you'll receive your e-tickets via email and in your CineMAS account.

Refund policies vary by theater. Generally, tickets can be refunded up to 2 hours before showtime. Some premium experiences may have different policies. Check the refund policy during checkout or contact our customer support for assistance.

Our rewards program lets you earn points for every ticket purchased. You earn 10 points per dollar spent, and these points can be redeemed for free tickets, concession stand discounts, movie merchandise, and exclusive movie experiences. Points expire after 12 months of inactivity.

Simply open the CineMAS and navigate to "My Tickets" section. Show the QR code to the theater attendant or scan it at the self-service kiosks available at most locations. No need to print physical tickets!
